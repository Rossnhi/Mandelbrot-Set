~ What is this? ~

This is a tool I made to observe and explore the mandelbrot set! You can watch a cool mandelbrot zoom video here.

To get you started, I have handpicked some co-ordinates for you to go to. Click on the menu in the top left section to have a look at them.

Here's how you can use this tool to explore the complex plane yourself:-

  • Zoom: The bigger the number the more zoomed in/ magnified will be the image.
  • Center: Where are you zooming in to? The center. you can enter your desired co-ordinates manually, there are some really cool looking points you can look up on the internet. Alternatively you can click anywhere on the canvas and the co-ordinates of that point will automatically be entered for you.
  • Iterations: More the iterations, better the quality and accuracy of the image. But when you are zoomed out, a high iteration count will be an overkill, plus it takes longer to process. Start out with a low iteration count, and as you zoom in more, increase it to get a clearer image. Take note that if you go above 1500-2000 it will take significantly longer time to process the image.

You can download an image of your cool looking discovery by clicking on the save image button. Happy exploring! :)
